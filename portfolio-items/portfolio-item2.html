Project Overview

This project's goal was to show dynamic content displayed on a webpage through the NY Times API. The website provides the most popular articles viewed/read on the NY Times website. The screenshot you see below was taken on 2/13/2021 and when you click the live demo, there will be new content. This is a website version but will show fine on a mobile device if zoomed out.
Also, each card shows it own:

  • Title and Picture
  • Date Published
  • Tag/Section
  • Brief summary
  • Link to article

There is always room for improvement and I seek to identify those key aspects in all of my projects. For this project, here's a few things that it will NOT do(but will be worked on):

  • Search bar functionality
  • Login/Signup
  • Filter Categories
